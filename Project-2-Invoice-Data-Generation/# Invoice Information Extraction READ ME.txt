# Invoice Information Extraction Annotation Project

## Objective
Annotate invoice-related text by identifying and labeling important invoice entities for Named Entity Recognition (NER).

## Labels Used

### Invoice Entities
- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Due Date
- Vendor
- Customer
- Amount
- Currency
- Tax
- GST
- Discount
- Purchase Order
- Order Number
- Payment Reference
- Payment Method
- Bank Name
- Outstanding Balance
- Payment Status
- Customer ID
- GSTIN
- Delivery Note
- Subtotal
- Shipping Charges
- Freight Charges
- Handling Charges
- TDS
- Cheque Number
- UTR Number
- Transaction ID

## Dataset
50 synthetic invoice-related sentences covering:
- Invoice creation
- Invoice payments
- Vendor and customer details
- Invoice and due dates
- Purchase orders
- Order numbers
- Payment methods (UPI, NEFT, RTGS, Bank Transfer)
- Payment references
- Bank names
- Invoice amounts
- Multiple currencies (INR, USD, EUR, GBP)
- Discounts
- Taxes and GST
- Shipping, freight, and handling charges
- Outstanding balances
- Payment status
- Customer IDs
- GSTIN numbers
- Delivery notes
- UTR numbers
- Cheque numbers
- Transaction IDs

## Tool Used
- Label Studio 1.23.0
